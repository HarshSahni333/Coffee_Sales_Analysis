# Executive Summary

## Overview

The Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard was developed to analyze sales performance across multiple coffee products, customer segments, and geographic markets between 2019 and 2022. The dashboard consolidates sales data into an interactive reporting environment, allowing users to explore trends and business performance through dynamic filters and visualizations.

## Key Business Findings

### Country Performance

The United States was the strongest-performing market, generating total sales of $35,639. This significantly exceeded revenue generated in Ireland and the United Kingdom, highlighting the United States as the primary contributor to overall business performance.

### Product Performance

Among all coffee varieties, Excelsa generated the highest sales revenue at $12,306. Liberica and Arabica followed closely, demonstrating strong demand across multiple product categories rather than reliance on a single product line.

### Sales Trends

Sales activity fluctuated throughout the observed period, with several noticeable peaks and declines. These variations suggest changing customer demand patterns and potential seasonal influences affecting purchasing behavior.

### Customer Analysis

The dashboard enables analysis of customer purchasing patterns through Loyalty Card segmentation and product size filters. These features provide valuable insight into customer preferences and purchasing behavior.

## Business Impact

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable business intelligence by:

* Providing visibility into sales performance trends
* Identifying top-performing products and markets
* Supporting strategic decision-making
* Enabling customer behavior analysis
* Improving reporting efficiency through interactive visualizations

## Conclusion

The Coffee Sales Analytics Dashboard demonstrates how Excel can be used as a business intelligence tool to convert transactional data into meaningful insights. Through interactive reporting and visual analysis, stakeholders can quickly identify trends, evaluate performance, and make informed business decisions.
